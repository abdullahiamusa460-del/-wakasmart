# WakaSmart 🚶

**Because the city belongs to everyone.**

An AI-powered navigation app that finds the smoothest routes for everyone—wheelchair users, parents with strollers, delivery riders, and everyone navigating Nigeria's unique terrain.

## The Problem

Standard GPS is built for cars, but Nigerians walk. For millions of students and citizens, the path is blocked by:

- 🔴 Deep open gutters and high curbs
- 🔴 Broken sidewalks and steep stairs without ramps
- 🔴 Flooded paths after rain and unlit streets at night

**The result**: A 5-minute trip for one person is a 30-minute struggle for a student in a wheelchair or a mother with a stroller.

## The Solution

Navigation with "Eyes" on the Ground.

WakaSmart is an AI-powered app that doesn't just find the shortest route—it finds the smoothest one.

### Key Features

- **🎯 Accessibility Filter**: Choose your mode (Wheelchair, Stroller, or "Avoid Mud")
- **⚡ Live Rerouting**: Avoids stairs, construction, and broken roads in real-time
- **📵 Offline Functionality**: Maps work even when data and network are unreliable
- **📸 Computer Vision**: Snap photos → AI categorizes roads instantly
- **📍 User Pings**: Real-time hazard reporting (like Waze)
- **📱 Vibration Sensing**: Auto-detect bumpy roads using phone sensors

## How It Works

### AI + Community

1. **Computer Vision**: Snap photos → AI categorizes roads (Sandy, High Curb, Accessible)
2. **User Pings**: Community reports hazards and working elevators in real-time
3. **Vibration Sensing**: Phone sensors detect bumpy roads automatically
4. **Offline Maps**: Core functionality works without internet

## Market Opportunity

We're targeting:

- 📊 **30 Million+ Nigerians** living with physical disabilities
- 👵 **The Elderly** who need to avoid long stairs and steep hills
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Parents** navigating busy markets or campuses with children
- 🚴 **Logistics & Delivery riders** needing the best paved routes

## Why WakaSmart Beats Google Maps

**Google sees the sky; WakaSmart sees the street.**

- Google Maps doesn't know where the gutters are
- It doesn't know if a sidewalk is broken or if a ramp exists
- WakaSmart is **Hyper-Local**—built specifically for Nigerian cities and campuses

## Business Model

- 🏫 **Campus Partnerships**: Sell "Smart Campus" licenses to universities
- ✅ **Verified Accessible Ads**: Local businesses pay to show as "WakaSmart Verified"
- 📊 **Government Data**: Provide "Road Health" data to city planners for infrastructure improvement

## Social Impact

This isn't just an app; it's a tool for justice. By making cities "walkable" for everyone, we ensure:

- Students with disabilities can get to class on time
- The elderly can move with dignity
- We're mapping a path to a more equal Nigeria
